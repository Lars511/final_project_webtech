Polderland Dutch Lexicon of Legal Terminology

The lexicon contains 6,207 Dutch words from the legal domain, comprising 4,781 nouns, 810 adjectives, 573 verbs, 12 adverbs and 31 items from other categories. It complies with the official Dutch Spelling (2005/6). Each entry contains an ID, word form and part of speech.The lexicon is delivered in human-readable UNIX ANSI-format, with just a linefeed at the end of every line instead of a carriage return. The character set used is ISO Latin-1 (ISO/IEC 8859-1). All fields are separated by horizontal tabs, with no empty fields.
