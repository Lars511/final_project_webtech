OLAC Coverage

Languages is the total number of languages in the given population range (from Ethnologue).
OLAC has data is the number of those languages that is represented by at least one resource in the OLAC collection (excluding the Ethnologue pages) and the next column, (%), expresses that as a percentage of the total for that population range.
Items is the total number of language resources for languages in the given population range (again, excluding the Ethnologue pages).
